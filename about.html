Who Is Garrett Phelps?

After seeing talented coaches burn out — not because they lacked passion, but because they lacked structure — Garrett built the operating systems he wished he'd had from day one.

The result is a coaching methodology that's clear, scalable, and teachable at any level. Whether you're running a D1 weight room or a one-person high school program, the frameworks work because they're built on principles, not personalities.

Garrett has trained 500+ coaches through his book, courses, and consulting — helping them get their time back, earn athlete buy-in faster, and build programs that run without constant firefighting.
