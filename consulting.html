The Real Problem

Your Program Runs You.
Not the Other Way Around.

Most athletic programs have talented coaches and willing athletes. What they don't have is a system that holds everything together without constant intervention. So everything depends on the person — not the process.

  • Your weight room operates on the head coach's memory. When they're gone, nothing is repeatable.
  • Athletes go through the motions because no one has given them a system they can actually buy into and measure their progress against.
  • Sport coaches have no idea what strength training is doing for their athletes — or why it matters — so buy-in breaks down in both directions.
  • You're running 4–8 different sports with zero coordination between them, which means your athletes are either undertrained, overtrained, or both at different points in the year.
  • Nobody can show the administration measurable proof that the performance program is producing results — so resources stay thin and the position stays marginal.
The Engagement

What You Get When We Build Together

This is not a seminar. It's not a template drop. It's a full-contact installation of a working performance system inside your specific program — built to your athletes, your staff, your constraints, and your culture.

01
Full Program Audit

We start by understanding what you have. Session flow, exercise selection, athlete tracking, staff communication, buy-in patterns — everything gets looked at before anything gets built. You can't fix what you haven't measured.

02
Custom Systems Architecture

Based on the audit, we build the systems your program actually needs: FYOP-aligned annual planning, sport-specific phase maps, Exercise Selection Funnels, progression standards, and athlete buy-in infrastructure. Built for your facility. Not a template. A system.

03
Staff Installation + Training

Systems only work if the people running them understand them. We train your staff on the frameworks — how to coach it, how to hold the standard, how to communicate it to athletes and sport coaches. The system has to live in the room, not just on paper.

04
Athlete Buy-In Infrastructure

We install the Athlete Buy-In Rate (ABIR) system inside your program — a measurable framework for tracking and improving athlete engagement. Coaches stop guessing why athletes are checked out and start managing the actual inputs that drive buy-in.

05
Data & Tracking System

Your program needs to be able to prove it's working. We build the tracking infrastructure — testing protocols, performance dashboards, progress reporting — so you can show athletes, sport coaches, and administrators measurable results.

06
90-Day Implementation Support

After the build, you're not on your own. We stay connected through the first 90 days of implementation — troubleshooting in real time, adjusting what needs adjusting, and holding the standard until the system runs itself.

The Process

How the Engagement Works

This engagement runs in four phases. Every phase has a clear deliverable. Nothing is open-ended. Nothing is vague. You'll know exactly what's being built and when.
